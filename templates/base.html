About this project

An independent, non-commercial research tool for browsing the catalogue of the LD Institute of Indology. Built to help students, scholars, and enthusiasts explore the holdings by topic, author, language, and era.

Source & credit

All catalogue metadata is compiled from the publicly accessible listing at ldindology.org. Full rights to the underlying manuscripts and their descriptions remain with the LD Institute of Indology, Ahmedabad.

Research use only

Presented here strictly for scholarly browsing and academic research. No copyright infringement is intended. If you are a rights holder and would like any entry removed or amended, please reach out and it will be taken down promptly.
